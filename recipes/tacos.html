Tacos Al Pastor

Description

Tacos al pastor is a quintessential Mexican dish, with tender pork and pineapple marinated in a savory and aromatic chile sauce. Serve with warm corn tortillas, tomatillos salsa, and lemon or lime wedges.

Ingredients

  • 1 tomato
  • 3 dried guajilla chile peppers
  • 2 dried ancho chli peppers
  • 1 pineapple, sliced 3/4 inch thick
  • 1/2 cup orange juice
  • 1 onion, quartered
  • 1/4 cup white vinegar
  • 2 chipolte peppers in adobo sauce
  • 1 tablespoon salt, or to taste
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 3 cloves
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 2 pounds boneless pork loin, thinly sliced

    • Steps

    1. Roast, peel, and de-seed the tomatoes. Boil and drain the peppers.
    2. Make the marinade in a blender.
