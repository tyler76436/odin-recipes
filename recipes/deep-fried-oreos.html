Deep Fried Oreos

Description

Deep-fried Oreos are a carnival favorite where I grew up. Oreos get dipped into pancake batter and fried â€” yummy! You can do this with or without a deep fryer.

Ingredients

  • Vegetable oil
  • Milk
  • An egg
  • Pancake mix
  • Oreos

Directions

  1. Make pancake batter
  2. Dip oreos in pancake batter
  3. Fry until golden brown
  4. Dust with powdered sugar and serve
